Use Cases

Digital Identity

Your HNS domain can act as a decentralized online ID. It works as a universal, cross-chain identifier for accessing all sorts of platforms and apps. You have full control over your online identity. Learn how to access your domain at NextDNS, HDNS, or HNS DoH.

Resistant Websites

Create censorship-resistant websites. Manage your DNS records and enable HTTPS access via DANE or upload your website to IPFS for decentralized hosting. Avoid the bureaucracy of ICANN and build on the only naming protocol with a truly decentralized root zone.

Wallet Naming

Get a human readable name to replace all of your lengthy crypto addresses. Stop risking typos and copy-pasting. Use your HNS domain to send and receive cryptocurrencies and NFTs. Transact between wallet addresses efficiently with an easily readable name.
